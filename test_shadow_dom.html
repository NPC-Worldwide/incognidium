Shadow DOM Pseudo-elements Demo

::part Pseudo-element

The ::part pseudo-element allows styling of shadow DOM parts from the outside:

  • my-element::part(button) - Styles the "button" part
  • my-element::part(header) - Styles the "header" part

::slotted Pseudo-element

The ::slotted pseudo-element styles content slotted into shadow DOM:

  • ::slotted(.highlight) - Styles elements with class "highlight"
  • ::slotted(*) - Styles all slotted elements
Note: These pseudo-elements are part of the CSS Shadow Parts and Scoping specifications. They enable encapsulation while allowing selective styling from outside the shadow root.

Shadow DOM pseudo-elements bridge the gap between encapsulation and customization, allowing component authors to expose specific styling hooks while keeping implementation details private.
